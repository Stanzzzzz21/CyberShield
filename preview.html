Some features:

Anti-Nuke

Neutralizes rogue staff and compromised accounts by instantly stripping permissions if they attempt to mass-delete channels or roles.

Invite Shield

Automatically purges unauthorized Discord invite links to prevent member poaching while allowing staff to share official connections.

Anti-Spam

Instantly neutralizes rapid-fire messaging, repetitive content, and phishing links with automated timeouts to keep your conversation clean.

Auto-Quarantine

Automatically isolates suspicious new accounts in a locked "holding" channel until they pass a manual or automated security verification.

Webhook Guard

Monitors and instantly deletes unauthorized webhooks, preventing "ghost pings" and hidden backdoors used by advanced raiders.
