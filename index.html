Last Updated: April 9, 2026

PART 1: PRIVACY POLICY

1. Introduction

CyberShield ("the Bot") is a security-focused service designed to protect Discord communities. This policy outlines how we handle data to ensure your server remains secure.

2. Data Collection

To provide anti-raid and server protection, the Bot processes the following:

  • Unique Identifiers: User IDs and Server IDs to manage permissions and moderation.
  • Action Logs: Discord Audit Logs to monitor mass-deletion of channels or roles (Anti-Nuke).
  • Invite Data: Metadata from invite links to prevent unauthorized poaching (Invite Shield).
  • Real-time Traffic: Message frequency and content patterns to detect rapid-fire messaging (Anti-Spam).

3. Data Usage & Sharing

  • No Selling: We never sell, trade, or share your data with third parties.
  • No Monitoring: We do not read or store private conversations for personal use.
  • Purpose: Data is used exclusively for automated security actions (kicks, bans, timeouts).

4. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Retention: Security logs and moderation data are automatically deleted after 30 days.
  • Removal: If the Bot is removed from a server, all configurations for that guild are wiped from our database.
  • Requests: Users may contact the developers to request a manual data deletion.
